The Elements
Symbolizing stability and groundedness, earth represents the material world—the solid ground beneath our feet. It embodies endurance, fertility, and the cycle of life.
Air whispers secrets through rustling leaves and carries our thoughts. It symbolizes freedom, communication, and the ever-flowing currents of existence.
Fiery passion and transformation define fire. It dances with intensity, consuming and renewing. Fire symbolizes creativity, illumination, and the spark of inspiration.
Fluid and adaptable, water mirrors our emotions. It flows, nourishes, and cleanses. Water embodies intuition, depth, and the ebb and flow of life's tides
